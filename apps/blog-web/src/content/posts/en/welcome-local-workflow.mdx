## Why this architecture

The older split between public web, admin, and API was fast to bootstrap, but the writing flow and the deployment boundary kept mixing together.

With the new structure, authoring ends locally and only `apps/blog-web` is deployed.

## What is already here

- language homes at `/ko/` and `/en/`
- static tag and category pages
- a language switcher
- frontmatter driven static generation

## What comes next

Once the local editor can export reviewed MDX with translation and scoring, the publishing workflow becomes much simpler.
